About Nepal

Nepal is a land of breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality. Nestled in the Himalayas, it is home to the world’s highest peak, Mount Everest, and offers travelers unforgettable experiences ranging from trekking and wildlife safaris to spiritual journeys.

Top Tourist Destinations

  • Pokhara: A lakeside city with stunning mountain views and adventure sports.
  • Chitwan National Park: Go on a jungle safari and spot rhinos and tigers.
  • Everest Base Camp: A trekker’s dream, offering incredible views of the world’s highest mountains.
  • Lumbini: The birthplace of Lord Buddha.
  • Bhaktapur & Patan: Ancient cities filled with traditional art, temples, and culture.

Cultural Highlights

Nepal boasts a vibrant blend of Hindu and Buddhist traditions. Colorful festivals like Dashain, Tihar, and Holi bring communities together in celebration. You’ll also find stunning temples, stupas, and rituals that are a testament to Nepal’s deep spiritual roots.

Travel Tips

  • Visa on arrival is available for most nationalities.
  • Best times to visit: March–May & September–November.
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly in temples.
  • Try local dishes like momo, dal bhat, and sel roti.
  • Always carry cash in rural areas—ATMs can be scarce.
